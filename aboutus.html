About Us

Our History

Depending on the size and field of your organization, we have different products to meet your requirements. We provide the optimum and customized solutions made for your organization.   GenX Enterprises is focusing exclusively in high quality and cost - effective product development. We are advancing on a tremendous pace and with involvement of skilled and experienced people working in the organization

To be the leading corrugated supplier providing our customers with value-add custom solution. We have the experience and capabilities to meet the unique corrugated packaging needs of our valued customers and create solutions that work !

Our mission is to establish a world – class corrugated box manufacturing company whose products will not only be used by both small and big corporations in the all sector in the India but also be exported to other countries of the world
